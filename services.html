    SERVICES

Owning a gigantic rodent may not be a piece of cake for everybody. That's why we are here to help you.

RENT-A-CAPY: Need a capybara for a special event? Our most requested service. We rent ours for up to 100 days at a time. Food not included.

TOURS: Visit our the sanctuary today. Petting area for the kids.

SPA: Treat your current capybara to our finest spa. Did you know they love water? We have a gigantic jacuzzi with 6 massage settings. Your pet will return home stress-free.
